Japanese Tonkatsu

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Instructions

Description

Tonkatsu, as you might have seen in various Japanese animes, is a popular menu in Japan. It is thinly sliced pork, breaded with and fried, and of course, very delicious.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced pork
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Panko

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet and oil it.
  2. Into the sliced pork, add eggs and flour and panko.
  3. Deep fry.
  4. Enjoy!
